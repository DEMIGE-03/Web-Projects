&#x20;           <h2>Background information</h2>

&#x20;           <p>The University of Dar es Salaam Computing Centre(UCC) is an Information and Communication Technology(ICT) Company owned by the University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM). UCC was established in 1999. UCC headquarter is located at UDSM's Julius Kambarage Nyerer Mlimani Campus. To ensure greater outreach to Tanzanians, UCC is having a branch in Dodoma, the Capital City of Tanzania.</p>
